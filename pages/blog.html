Excerpt from Half Girlfriend

Half Girlfriend is a story of middle class boy who fall in love with the highher class girl and the both have different lifestyles. The story revolves around a boy who comes from the lower class family and the girl with whom he is in love with. At the beginning of the story the girl rejects the proposal as she thinks they are good friends. These two are selected throught the sports program and they become very close friends as they play for same basketball association. The boy tries to make her his girlfriend but she refuses and she faked about she was sufferibg with cancer. After three years, the boy came to know about she is alive and she had faked her cancer. At last he finds her again and they both get married each other

---Chetan Bhagat

Chetan Bhagat is an Indian author. His novel focus on youth and urban India, that makes him very popular among audiences.
